About Me

I'm Shane Foster, a cinematographer and actor born on June 30, 1977, in Vallejo, California. Standing at 6'2" (1.88m), I bring both technical expertise and on-screen presence to every project. My passion is creating compelling visual narratives through skilled cinematography while also contributing as a performer when the role demands it.

With over 15 years of experience in the film industry, I've worked across multiple departments including camera operations, lighting, and grip work. My versatility allows me to understand productions from multiple perspectives, making me a valuable collaborator on any set.
